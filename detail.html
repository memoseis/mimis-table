Pear, cinnamon & sweet walnut porridge

This creamy pear, cinnamon & walnut porridge is the perfect winter breakfast! It takes just ten minutes or so to make and it’s full of flavour.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe pear
  • 100g of porridge oats, 2/3 cup
  • 250ml almond milk, 1 cup
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • Handful of roasted walnuts, chopped
